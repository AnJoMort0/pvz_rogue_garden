#Mutation Plant from the [[Basic Synergy Deck]].

Mutated by evolution from an [[Torchwood]].

Daytime affinity: #Ambivalent 
Ground affinity: #Ground 

Speciality:
	Pea projectiles passing through the Poison Oak are coated in poison
	Poison level 1: Poisonous fumes surround the projectile, and deal damage directly to the zombie, ignoring armour
	Poison level 2: The projectile is coated in liquid poison, which poisons the hit zombie on impact
