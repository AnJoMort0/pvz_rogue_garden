#Mutation Plant from the [[Basic Night Deck]].

Mutated by evolution from a [[Sun-shroom]].

Daytime affinity: #Night
Ground affinity: #Ground 

Speciality:
	Has a limited attack range. Eat a single zombie in its melee area, instantly devouring it. Cool down between each zombie eaten.
	When it is ready to eat again, it also instantly produces sun.
	If the zombie is too big to eat, licks at it to deal damage.
