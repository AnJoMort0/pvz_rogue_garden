#Mutation Plant from the [[Basic Night Deck]].

Mutated by evolving from a [[Hypno-shroom]]

Daytime affinity: #Night 
Ground affinity: #Aerial 

Speciality:
	Shoots a ray at the front-most zombie of its lane or neighbouring lanes. The hit zombie is vanquished and a [[Puff-shroom]] is planted on that tile if possible.
	Can target plants turned into sheep, using its attack to turn them back.
	Cool down between attacks.
