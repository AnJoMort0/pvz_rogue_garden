#Mutation Plant from the [[Basic Day Deck]].

Mutated by evolution from a [[Chomper]].

Daytime affinity: #Day
Ground affinity: #Ground 

Speciality:
	Has a limited attack range. Eat a single zombie in its melee area, instantly devouring it. Cool down between each zombie eaten.
	When it is ready to eat again, it also heals the plant in the tile in front of itself.
	If the zombie is too big to eat, bites at it to deal damage.
