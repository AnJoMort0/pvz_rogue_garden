#Mutation Plant from the [[Basic Night Deck]].

Mutated by evolution from a [[Fume-shroom]]

Daytime affinity: #Night
Ground affinity: #Ground 

Placement speciality: When planted on the roof, can target zombies on the garden.

Speciality:
	Lobs spores down its lane.
	If a mushroom in its lane or neighbouring lanes is vanquished, makes a shock wave attack at the zombie that vanquished it.
