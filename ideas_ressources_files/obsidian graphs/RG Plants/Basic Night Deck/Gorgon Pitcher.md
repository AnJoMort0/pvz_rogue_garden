#Mutation Plant from the [[Basic Night Deck]].

Mutated by evolution from a [[Grave Buster]].

Daytime affinity: #Ambivalent 
Ground affinity: #Ground 

Placement speciality: When shovelled or vanquished, the obstacle originally on that tile remains there.

Speciality:
	Shoots a petrifying beam down its lane, turning a zombie into a rough statue. Plants can still be placed on that tile.
	The rough statue effectively acts as a wall that the zombies have to eat through. The health of the statue is based on the health of the transformed zombie.
	Gorgon Pitcher can only sustain one rough statue at a time, so it cannot attack again until its statue is destroyed.
	If it attempts to petrify a zombie it cannot, it instead stuns it.
