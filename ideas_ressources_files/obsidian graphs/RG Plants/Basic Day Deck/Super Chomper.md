#Mutation Plant from the [[Basic Day Deck]].

Mutated by evolution from a [[Chomper]].

Daytime affinity: #Day
Ground affinity: #Ground 

Speciality:
	Has a limited attack range. Eats up to five zombie in its melee area at once, instantly devouring them. Cool down between each group of zombies eaten.
	If the zombie is too big to eat, bites at it to deal damage.
