#Mutation Plant from the [[Basic Night Deck]].

Mutated by evolution from a [[Puff-shroom]]

Daytime affinity: #Night
Ground affinity: #Ground 

Speciality:
	Has a limited attack range. Shoots spores in a straight line down its lane.
	Buffs mushrooms in a 3x3 area centred on the tile it is planted, increasing their damage and preventing [[Scaredy-shroom]]s from hiding.
