#Mutation Plant from the [[Basic Synergy Deck]].

Mutated by evolution from an [[Spikeweed]].

Daytime affinity: #Ambivalent 
Ground affinity: #Ground 

Placement speciality: Can only be placed in true ground tiles, no flower pots or lily pads.

Speciality: Ignored by zombies, which can traverse it. Deals damage to zombies on its tile.
