Plant from the [[Instant Plants Wheel]].

Daytime affinity: #Ambivalent 
Ground affinity: #Ground 

Speciality: Placed on a sun producer, which is consumed upon the Magnifying Grass' placement. Deals damage to one zombie at a time, with a large beam of light the duration of which is defined by the sun producer it consumes, and the power of which is defined by the number of sun producers in a 2x5 area behind itself.
